The best movies according to Samuel

My top 3 movies

Powder

Possibly my most loved movie of all time, everything from the acting to the musical score is absolutely top notch.
Highly recommended.

Boondock Saints

Another excellent movie boasting a star studded cast and outstanding musical score, action packed, funny, emotional.
Worth noting that Sean Patrick Flanery is in the above 2 movies.

Interstellar

Once again, the soundtrack is worth mentioning here, the composition is second to none.
The story itself is compelling and relevent in this day and age, it's easy to sympathise with the chatacters thanks to the amazing effort of the actors.
It has been mentioned that the digital representation of the blackhole has been called "A Triumph of Visual Effects and Theoretical Physics".
